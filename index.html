I make things that (usually) don't break.

Every since I was a kid, I have been fascinated with how the world works. This led to an early interest in physics, which eventually made me fall in love with building things (engineering). A guiding principle or motto I have for my life is "do hard things". I try to apply this to both my personal and professional life. Over the past few years this has meant climbing a 14er after only being in Colorado for 3 weeks, building a spacecraft thermal model from scratch, and serving as a student leader for a study abroad trip in a foreign country. Overall, I am always up for a challenge and welcome learning opportunities.
